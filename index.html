Megh Bhalerao

I am an incoming Master's in Electrical Engineering Student in the University of Washington, Seattle. My research interests are understanding the theory and applications of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision with a special interest in applications to Biomedical Datasets.

I was a Project Assistant at the Image Analysis and Compter Vision Lab, EE Department, at the Indian Institute of Science with Prof. Soma Biswas. I worked on Domain Adaptation in Computer Vision using Deep Learning.

Previously (2019), I worked on Deep Learning Semantic Segmentation of Medical Images at the Center of Biomedical Image Computing and Analysis, University of Pennsylvania with Dr. Spyridon Bakas as a part of my Undergraduate thesis. During my undergraduate studies, I was fortunate to be advised by Dr. CMC Krishnan.

I graduated with a B.Tech in EE from NITK, Surathkal (2020).
